Privacy Policy

Baraka Furnitures — Last updated: June 2026

Baraka Furnitures ("we", "our", "us") operates a furniture retail business based in Nairobi, Kenya. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with us through WhatsApp, social media, or our other communication channels.

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us via WhatsApp or our advertising channels, we may collect:

  • Your name and phone number
  • Messages, photos, and voice notes you send us
  • Your delivery address and location details you provide
  • Order and purchase information
  • Information about which advertisement brought you to us

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer service
  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Share product information, prices, and photos you request
  • Follow up on enquiries and confirm deliveries
  • Improve the quality of our customer service

3. Automated Responses

Some of our WhatsApp responses are powered by automated assistant technology to serve you faster. Your conversations may be processed by secure third-party AI services to generate helpful responses. A member of our team is always available — you can request to speak with a person at any time.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share information with:

  • Delivery riders and courier services (your name, phone number, and delivery address — only as needed to deliver your order)
  • Service providers that help us operate our messaging systems (such as Meta's WhatsApp Business Platform), under their respective privacy terms

5. Data Storage & Security

Your conversation data is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary to serve you. Order records are kept for business and accounting purposes. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

In line with the Kenya Data Protection Act (2019), you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of follow-up messages at any time by replying "STOP"
