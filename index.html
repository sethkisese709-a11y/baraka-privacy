Privacy Policy

Luxecraft Furnitures · Last updated: June 2026

Luxecraft Furnitures ("we", "our", "us") operates a furniture retail business based in Nairobi, Kenya. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with us through WhatsApp, social media, or our other communication channels.

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us via WhatsApp or our advertising channels, we may collect:

  • Your name and phone number
  • Messages, photos, and voice notes you send us
  • Your delivery address and location details you provide
  • Order and purchase information
  • Information about which advertisement brought you to us

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and assist with purchases
  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To follow up on pending orders or quotes you requested
  • To improve the quality of our customer service

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as needed to operate the service — for example with delivery partners to fulfil your order, and with technology providers (such as messaging and AI service providers) that help run our assistant on our behalf. These providers are required to protect your information.

4. Data Retention

We keep conversation and order information only as long as needed to serve you and meet our business and legal obligations, after which it is deleted.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of follow-up messages at any time by replying "STOP"
